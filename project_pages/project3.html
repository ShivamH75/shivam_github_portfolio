Power BI Dashboard: Electric Vehicle Population

Overview

This project aims to create a comprehensive Power BI dashboard for analyzing electric vehicle (EV) data. The dataset includes detailed information on electric vehicles, such as their location, make, model, type, and various other attributes. The dashboard will provide insights into the distribution, characteristics, and trends of electric vehicles across different counties and cities.

Dataset

The data for this project is sourced from Kaggle (Ref. dataset).

Sr. No. Entity Description
1. VIN Vehicle Identification Number (unique identifier)
2. County The county where the vehicle is registered
3. City The city where the vehicle is registered
4. State The state where the vehicle is registered
5. Postal Code The postal code of the vehicle's location
6. Model Year The year the vehicle model was manufactured
7. Make The manufacturer of the vehicle
8. Model The specific model of the vehicle
9. Electric Vehicle Type The type of electric vehicle (e.g., Battery Electric Vehicle, Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle)
10. CAFV Eligibility Indicates if the vehicle is eligible for Clean Alternative Fuel Vehicle incentives
11. Electric Range The electric range of the vehicle in miles
12. Base MSRP The Manufacturer's Suggested Retail Price (MSRP) of the vehicle
13. Legislative District The legislative district where the vehicle is registered
14. DOL Vehicle ID The Department of Licensing Vehicle ID
15. Vehicle Location The geographic coordinates (latitude and longitude) of the vehicle's location
16. Electric Utility The electric utility provider(s) servicing the vehicle's location
17. 2020 Census Tract The census tract from the 2020 Census where the vehicle is located

Expected Outcomes

  • A user-friendly Power BI dashboard providing valuable insights into the distribution and characteristics of electric vehicles.

  • Enhanced decision-making capabilities for policymakers and stakeholders based on comprehensive EV data analysis.

  • Identification of trends and patterns in electric vehicle adoption, helping to inform future strategies for promoting clean transportation.
